Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a package?
Booking a package is easy! Simply browse our Destinations or Tour Packages, select the one you love, and click "Plan Trip" or "Book Now". You'll be guided through a simple form to customize your dates, passengers, and preferences. Our team will verify availability and send you a secure payment link.
Can I customize a trip?
Absolutely! We specialize in curated, customizable experiences. When you submit an inquiry, let our travel experts know what specific experiences, hotels, or pacing you prefer, and we will tailor the itinerary perfectly to your needs.
What payment options are available?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking, and direct bank transfers. We also offer flexible EMI options on select premium packages to make your dream holiday more accessible.
How do cancellations work?
Cancellations are subject to the specific terms of your package. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days in advance receive a full refund minus a small processing fee. Cancellations made closer to the travel date may incur partial charges depending on hotel and flight provider policies. Reach out to our support team for help with your specific booking.
Do you offer international tours?
Yes! We offer curated international packages to premium destinations like the Maldives, Dubai, Vietnam, Thailand, and Europe. Our international packages include comprehensive support for visa assistance and international flight bookings.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team 24/7 via WhatsApp, email us at support@campfly.com, or call our toll-free number during business hours. Scroll down to the Contact Support section for direct links!
